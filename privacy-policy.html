Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Mail Server Report Generator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Google Account Information

  • We access your Google account email address for authentication purposes only.
  • We do not store your password or any login credentials.

Google Drive Access

  • We request access to Google Drive to:
    • Create folders for organizing reports
    • Upload report documents to your Drive
    • Read folder structure (read-only access)
  • We only access folders and files that you explicitly select or create through our application.
  • We do not access, read, or modify any other files in your Google Drive.

Report Data

  • All report data (including text, timestamps, and screenshots) is processed locally in your browser.
  • Report data is only uploaded to your Google Drive when you explicitly click "Upload to Drive."
  • We do not store, transmit, or have access to your report data except when uploading to your Drive.

Local Storage

  • We use browser local storage to:
    • Save draft reports locally on your device
    • Remember your preferences (dark mode, folder settings)
  • This data never leaves your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and allow access to Google Drive
  • Report Generation: To create and organize reports in your Google Drive
  • User Preferences: To remember your settings and improve your experience

Data Storage and Security

  • No Server Storage: We do not operate any servers. All processing happens in your browser.
  • Google Drive: Reports are stored in your Google Drive, which you control.
  • Local Storage: Draft data is stored only on your device.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your data with any third parties.

Google APIs

This application uses Google APIs and adheres to:

  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Your Rights

  • Access: You can access all your data through Google Drive
  • Deletion: You can delete any reports or folders created by this application
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke Google Drive access at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear browser local storage to remove draft data

Cookies and Tracking

  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking services

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
